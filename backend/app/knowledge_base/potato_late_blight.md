# Potato Late Blight

## Disease name
Potato Late Blight

## Crop
Potato

## Symptoms
- Visible spots, discoloration, curling, or drying depending on crop condition.
- Reduced leaf health and possible impact on plant growth.

## Causes
- Pathogen pressure, humidity, poor airflow, infected residues, or vector spread.

## Treatment
- Remove heavily infected leaves where appropriate.
- Improve irrigation and airflow.
- Consult a local agriculture expert before chemical treatment.

## Prevention
- Use disease-free planting material.
- Monitor fields regularly.
- Maintain spacing, sanitation, and crop rotation.

## Severity
Medium to High depending on spread and crop stage.
